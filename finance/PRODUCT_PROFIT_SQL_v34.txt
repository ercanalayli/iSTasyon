AperiON urun satis SMM kar veri modeli v34

Tablo 1 product_costs
- company
- product_code
- product_name
- category
- cost_date
- unit_cost
- source_type
- active

Tablo 2 product_sales_profit
- company
- sale_date
- invoice_no
- cari_name
- product_code
- product_name
- quantity
- unit_sale_price
- unit_cost
- sales_amount
- cogs_amount
- gross_profit
- dynamic_expense_share
- variable_expense
- net_profit
- net_margin
- profit_status

Formul
sales_amount = quantity x unit_sale_price
cogs_amount = quantity x unit_cost
gross_profit = sales_amount - cogs_amount
net_profit = gross_profit - dynamic_expense_share - variable_expense
net_margin = net_profit / sales_amount x 100

Kontrol kurallari
1 Urun satisinda unit_cost bos veya 0 ise kar guven skoru duser.
2 Hizmet satisi ayri kurala tabidir; hizmette SMM yoktur.
3 Urun satisinda SMM zorunludur.
4 Degisken gider varsa net kar anlik yeniden hesaplanir.
5 Sabit gider payi satis tutari, brut kar, adet veya kategoriye gore dagitilir.
